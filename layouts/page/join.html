How to sign-up

Choose your membership plan below — if you opt to pay for the whole year in advance you will get 12 months for the price of 11. Click on the appropriate "Join" button and this will take you to the Direct Debit payment form. Complete the Direct Debit sign-up and then you will be redirected to another form to provide your personal details for our records. You must complete both parts of the sign-up for your membership to be complete.

If you have any queries or problems, please email membership@invictatouch.com (please allow up to 48 hours for a response).
