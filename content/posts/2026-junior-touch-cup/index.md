Playing in the under-12s boys division despite being a mixed team, and with an average age of just
9.7 years, the Invicta youngsters won their first ever match and enjoyed competitive games against
other teams from the region, eventually finishing 6th out of 9 teams.

The Invicta team featured Darwin, Avery, William M, Bethan, William D, Oscar, Max, Liam, Nathan, and
Betsy. They were supported by coaches Tom and Antoni, and referee Alex.
