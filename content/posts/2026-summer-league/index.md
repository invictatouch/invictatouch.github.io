summary:
  The first Canterbury touch league was played in 2006 and it has been played every summer since
  except &mdash; due to pandemic restrictions &mdash; 2020. That makes 2026 both the 20th
  anniversary and the 20th edition of the league.
---
This year we once again have 16 mixed-gender teams playing each week with over 150 players old and
new involved. For many years one of these teams has been [Burgess Hodgson](https://burgesshodgson.co.uk)
and we are delighted that they are also our league sponsor this year.

The first competitive games were played on Wednesday and you can find the results and standings
[here]({{<relref "/league/2026/" >}}). Defending champions the Wombles are aiming for a third title
in a row but they face stiff competition after narrowly losing their opening match.

After seven weeks of round-robin games the top four in each division will progress to the
semi-finals. The finals will be played on Wednesday 15th July.
