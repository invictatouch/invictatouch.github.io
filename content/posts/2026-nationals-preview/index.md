The Nationals features regional teams from around the country competing in open, seniors, and
masters divisions over three days at the University of Nottingham.
The South East is represented by the *Sharks* and the *Taipans*, combining the best players from
clubs in London, Kent, Sussex, and Surrey. This year seven Invicta members have been selected in
these teams. In addition, a few of our members who are currently living further afield will be
representing other regions.

The full schedule of matches is [here](https://tournifyapp.com/live/etanat2026/) and more information
about the tournament and the teams involved can be found in the [online event programme](https://canva.link/etanat2026).
Games on field 1 (all three days) and field 9 (Monday only) will be live-streamed on the
[England Touch Youtube channel](https://www.youtube.com/englandtouch).
The following Invicta players will be involved and their live-streamed games, including any potential
finals, have been highlighted below.

#### Women's Open
**Lydia Ticehurst** and her South East Sharks teammates are aiming to retain the title
they won 12 months ago. They will also face the French national team in a friendly match.

:tv: Sunday 9:50 &middot; South East Sharks vs. France \
:tv: Monday 11:30 &middot; Semi-Finals \
:tv: Monday 15:10 &middot; Final

----

#### Men's Open
**Vaughn Meredith** is once again representing the South East Taipans in the men's open division
as they aim to regain the title they last won in 2023. **Gus Lister** is assistant coach. The
Taipans will also take on France and Ireland in friendly matches.

:tv: Monday 12:25 &middot; Semi-Finals \
:tv: Monday 16:05 &middot; Final

----

#### Mixed Open
There is significant Invicta interest in the mixed division with **Matthew Mayall** and **Eliot
Nye** in the Taipans team while **Toby Lister** defends the title he won last year with the
South West Saxons. Toby is joined in the Saxons side by **Danny Ticehurst** and will have to
overcome his brother **JJ Lister**'s Midland Tigers if he is to be successful again. Each of
the mixed open regional teams will also play a friendly against the French mixed open team.


:tv: Sunday 10:40 &middot; South West Saxons vs. South East Sharks \
:tv: Monday 9:00 &middot; Midland Tigers vs. South East Sharks \
:tv: Monday 14:15 &middot; Final

----

#### Men's 40s
**Mike Bailey** and **Dan Dyer** won the 40s division with the South East Taipans in 2025 and
they return to defend their title this year. They will also play a friendly against the Scottish
national team.

:tv: Saturday 10:50 &middot; South East Taipans vs. South West Saxons \
:tv: Sunday 16:55 &middot; Final

----

#### Men's 50s
The men's 50s is the only division that the South East has never won at the Nationals.
**Martin Reynard** will be looking to put that right with his Sharks team. They will also take on
the Scotland men's 50s side in a friendly.

:tv: Monday 16:05 &middot; Final
